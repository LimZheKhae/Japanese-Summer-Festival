Enjoy fun mini games.



Ningyo Sukui (toy scooping)

Probably need no explanation, but this is a game where you scoop little toys made of rubber or plastic. They usually have popular characters from Disney or Pokemon, so it's a really popular game mostly among children.

As a traveller, immersing yourself in Japanese culture and customs is a mesmerising experience that will leave you with lifelong memories! The good news for visitors to Japan is that There are several opportunities to learn from and interact with locals. Few nations are as warmly inviting as Japan, and while the Japanese appreciate travellers' attempts to respect their culture, they are also patient and willing to help foreigners understand. In terms of cross-cultural learning, Japan is an awesome country to visit. Join us as we learn about Japanese culture.
